Hey, I'm Max

Cinematographer and Colorist based in Munich with a passion for visual storytelling.

What drives me? Finding the perfect connection between technology and creativity. As a cinematographer, I capture moments that touch and move people. As a colorist, I give them their final emotional depth. This unique combination allows me to guide projects from the first vision to the last color tone.

Whether commercial, brand film, social media spot, or music video – I support directors in bringing their stories to life visually. I’m both a technical expert and creative problem solver. Challenges? Welcome! Unconventional solutions? My specialty!

When I’m not behind the camera or working on color grading, I find creative inspiration baking sourdough bread – a process that has surprisingly many parallels to my film work: patience, precision, and the joy of the perfect result.

Got a project in mind? Or interested in working together? Just use the contact form, send me an email, or give me a call. I'd love to hear from you.
