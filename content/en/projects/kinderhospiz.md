The Children’s Hospice St. Nikolaus accompanies families with a child or adolescent suffering from an incurable and life-limiting illness during the illness and also during the dying and grieving phases. However, it is not only a place of grief but also a place that allows imaginations to thrive. The goal is to provide affected families with a piece of quality of life. I had the privilege of supporting my colleague Maximilian Rödl in the camera department and color grading for this incredible project. This cinema spot was created over three shooting days.
